Lena Horne Hall (2045)

Named after legendary singer and actress Lena Horne — and located on the same block as her childhood home — the museum and sculpture garden features statues, artifacts, and rare footage from important cultural and political figures that hail from or have strong connections to Bed-Stuy, including Aaliyah, Norah Jones, Chris Rock, and Bobby Fischer. An important cultural landmark for locals and a must-see for NYC tourists, explore this unique celebration of the neighborhood, its people, and the outsized impact its had on the world.
